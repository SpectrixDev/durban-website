Popular Destinations

Discover the most popular destinations in Durban and immerse yourself in the city's unique culture and experiences.

uShaka Marine World

Explore the largest aquarium in the Southern Hemisphere, where you can get up close with dolphins, sharks, and other marine life. Enjoy the thrilling water rides and dine in one of the many restaurants on the premises.

Moses Mabida Stadium

Take in the stunning views of Durban from the top of this iconic stadium. For the adrenaline junkies, try the Big Swing or bungee jump off the stadium's arch.

Durban central: Nightlife heaven

Experience Durban's vibrant nightlife in the city center. With a variety of bars, clubs, and restaurants to choose from, there's something for everyone. Walk around and see what the city has to offer.
